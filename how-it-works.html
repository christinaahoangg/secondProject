How It Works

  • 01 Select Your Plan

    Select a plan that suits your health goals, and select your meals weekly.


  • 02 Delivered to Your Door

    Chef created meals using fresh local ingredients are delivered to your door.


  • 03 Heat and Eat

    Heat up your nutritious meals in just minutes whenever you're ready to eat.

Our Food

Our team of nutritionists and chefs will work closely every week to develop a total nutrient diet that combines all the micro and large nutrients you need to meet your daily recommended intake of three meals a day! We track more than 30 nutrients from protein to zinc in your diet, providing you with all the important components of a healthy diet.

Our Plans

You can eat as much as you want, at $10.40 per meal. We have breakfast, lunch, dinner and snacks. Tell us about your limitations or allergies and we will make sure to adjust the diet accordingly. We offer different types of diet, such as low carbs and ketones. Choose the steam that suits your lifestyle. Once you select your stream, you can set your own meal schedule. If you don't like the menu for that day, you can choose from our seasonal menu. The more meals you buy, the more money you save per meal. Please see our pricing plan.

Delivery

Fresh ready to eat food will be delivered to your door every other day, effortlessly. For home delivery, you will receive a text message indicating that your delivery is on the way. You can wait for your driver at the door, or we can park at your door. All the bags are filled with ice bags, so your food will be very fresh. We deliver goods all over the next continent. See the delivery area below. If you are not in our delivery area and you are interested in getting food, please contact us, we will contact you after expanding to your area. Delivery to your home is between 5 p.m. and 10 p.m. on Sunday / Tuesday / Thursday, and we will deliver directly from the kitchen to you in the next two days. For office delivery, our delivery window will depend on office hours and access.
